MAX_WAV

The max size of the "data" chunk of a WAVE file. This is the max unsigned 32-bit int value, minus 100 bytes (overkill, 44 would be safe) for the header. From https://github.com/TooTallNate/node-wav/blob/master/lib/writer.js
